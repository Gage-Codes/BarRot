About the Podcast

Come find a spot at the bar. We have some shit to talk about.

Welcome to Bar Rot! The late-night podcast where we discuss nightlife, bad decisions, friendship, dating, work, the chaos of the industry, and how Terry keeps calling out and still has a job.

Join us for our post-shift therapy session, some trash talk, and a few good laughs. Bar Rot is for anyone who regrets saying “one more shot”... and for those of us who pour them.

Episode 01

Neat or Chilled

Recipe

  • 2 oz. Whiskey, Tequila, Vodka, or Rum
  • Ice, optional
  • A long day

Method

  1. Choose your liquor.
  2. Either pour it straight into a glass, or shake it with ice and strain into a fresh glass.
  3. Go ahead and slam it.
  4. Congrats! You just had your first of many shift drinks.
