The Culture

Come find a spot at the bar. We have some shit to talk about.

Welcome to Bar Rot! The late-night podcast where we talk nightlife, bad decisions, friendship, dating, work, the chaos of the industry, and how Terry keeps calling out and still has a job.

Bar Rot was founded by three friends who, by chance, have all worked with each other at different times in their lives. Together, the three of them have decades' worth of hospitality experience ranging from restaurants, dive bars, cocktail lounges, and nightclubs. One drunken night after work, the idea for Bar Rot was born and what started as a conversation fueled by tequila soon became a podcast.

So join us for our post-shift therapy session, some trash talk, and a few good laughs. Bar Rot is for anyone who regrets saying “one more shot”... and especially for the people who pour them.

Shifties

Every episode begins with a cocktail and ends with a story.

Choose an episode, make the drink, and unwind with us. It's been one hell of a day, and we all deserve a drink.

Episode 01

Neat or Chilled

Recipe

  • 2 oz. Whiskey, Tequila, Vodka, or Rum
  • Ice, optional
  • A long day

Method

  1. Choose your liquor.
  2. Either pour it straight into a glass, or shake it with ice and strain into a fresh glass.
  3. Go ahead and slam it.
  4. Congrats! You just had your first of many shift drinks.
